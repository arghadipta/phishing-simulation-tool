How to Spot Phishing in the Future:

Before Clicking:

  • Verify the sender's full email address
  • Look for spelling and grammar errors
  • Question unexpected or urgent requests
  • Hover over links to see real destination
  • Don't trust email display names alone

When in Doubt:

  • Contact the sender through a known method
  • Don't call phone numbers in suspicious emails
  • Type website URLs manually, don't click links
  • Report suspicious emails to IT security
  • Never provide credentials via email links
