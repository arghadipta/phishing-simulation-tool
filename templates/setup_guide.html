Quick Start (5 Minutes)

Step 1: Create a Campaign

  1. Click "Create New Campaign" on the dashboard
  2. Enter a campaign name (e.g., "Q1 2024 Security Training")
  3. Select a phishing template
  4. Add target users (format: email@domain.com Name)
  5. Click "Create Campaign"

Step 2: Get Tracking Links

  1. You'll be redirected to the campaign detail page
  2. Each target has a unique tracking link
  3. These links are how you track who clicks

Step 3: Send Phishing Emails

  1. Go to Email Templates
  2. Copy the template matching your campaign
  3. Replace [TRACKING_LINK] with each person's unique link
  4. Send the emails to your targets

Step 4: Monitor Results

  1. Return to your campaign detail page
  2. Watch real-time statistics update
  3. See who clicked and who submitted credentials
  4. Review the activity timeline

Step 5: Follow Up with Training

  1. Schedule a training session within 1-2 weeks
  2. Share anonymized results with the team
  3. Review the red flags people missed
  4. Provide resources for improvement
  5. Recognize those who reported the phishing attempt

Best Practices

  • Start Easy: Use easy templates first, increase difficulty over time
  • Run Regularly: Quarterly campaigns maintain awareness
  • Vary Templates: Don't use the same template repeatedly
  • Timing Matters: Tuesday-Thursday mornings work best
  • Avoid Stress Periods: Skip holidays, year-end, major projects
  • Make it Educational: Focus on learning, not catching people
  • Celebrate Success: Recognize those who report phishing
  • Track Improvement: Compare campaigns over time

Understanding the Metrics

Click Rate: Percentage who clicked the phishing link

  • Good: < 10%
  • Needs Improvement: 10-30%
  • Critical: > 30%

Submit Rate: Percentage who entered credentials

  • Good: < 5%
  • Needs Improvement: 5-10%
  • Critical: > 10%
